About Us

Agnitio Counseling was founded by former Texas public high school students, well-versed in today's competitive college application atmosphere, with a passionate focus on your student’s achievement. Our team is committed to empowering driven and talented young minds to optimize their potential by leveraging our proven experience in gaining admission to the Ivy League, various top 25 universities, and prestigious scholarship programs. Agnitio aims to guide hardworking and dedicated scholars and assist in crafting applications that elevate their journey towards admission into their dream institutions and programs.

Agnitio stands out from traditional college counseling programs by incorporating a modern and personalized approach with a team of students who were just in your shoes. We understand how anxiety-inducing and intimidating it can be to look ahead toward all the work that needs to be completed between now and your application deadlines, but with the guidance of our team it all becomes (a little) more bearable. With one-on-one guidance counseling and planning, a workload broken down into more centralized packages to be edited by our team, and various mentorship and oversight opportunities, Agnitio will be there for you!

With the first application cycle, Agnitio is offering a FREE counseling program for select students. We believe that every highly motivated student deserves a chance to reach their academic and professional goals, and our team is dedicated to providing invaluable guidance and support to improve accessibility into the nation’s most prestigious and well-suited universities.

Services

Guidance and Resume Counseling

We understand all the challenges that must be overcome when you first begin to formulate your college list and build a resume that will attract top schools. As a group of recent admits who have navigated all that encapsulates the modern-day college application process, our team offers one-on-one question and answer style sessions for students to receive personalized advice and support that is proven to guide them towards their perfect fit!

What to Expect:

  • Suggestions on college lists
  • Advice on resume building
  • Help with formatting extracurriculars
  • Insight into various programs and universities

Personal Essay Consulting and Editing

Your personal statement is one of the most important elements to your college application. Because of its pivotal position in the holistic review of your application, the personal essay is emphasized by our program. It is a vital part of your application that should build a narrative of both who you are and how you will make an impact when admitted. Because of its importance, we dedicate more time to it in our program, consisting of a one-on-one planning session as well as an editing session to ensure we highlight all your strengths and encompass your character.

What to Expect:

  • Format and topic suggestions
  • Grammar editing and enhancement
  • Personalized revisions

Supplemental Essay Editing

As you dive further into your college application process, it can become difficult to display researched interest into select schools through your supplemental essays while standing out as an applicant. To help better format these essays, our team offers online editing services for your supplemental writing. Guided by extensive experience and knowledge on various universities, our team provides insightful revisions to help you match your dream program.

What to Expect:

  • Suggested, school-specific revisions
  • Grammar editing and enhancement

Committee Overview

As a conclusion to your experience with Agnitio, our team will come together to do a holistic review of each application for a university of the student’s choice. By factoring in elements of our own applications, as well as extensive research and insight from people involved in the admissions process, our team will take on the lenses of an admissions officer and give a review followed by last pieces of advice to ensure your application is the best it can be prior to submission for real evaluation.

What to Expect:

  • Assessments on your strongest/weakest application aspects
  • Final remarks and advice
